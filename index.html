Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This app — Thought Time — does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, because it doesn't need to. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What the app stores, and where

The app stores your meditation sessions, your before/after thoughts, and your saved presets entirely on your own device, using local on-device storage. None of this data is sent anywhere. There is no server, no account, and no login — the app does not have the technical ability to transmit your data even if it wanted to.

What the app does not do

  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising or ad networks
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • No account creation, no email, no personal identifiers requested
  • No location access
  • No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to anyone, for any reason

Deleting your data

Uninstalling the app deletes all of its locally stored data along with it. There is no account to delete elsewhere, because none exists.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data from anyone at all.

Changes to this policy

If this app's data practices ever change in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect that before any change takes effect.
